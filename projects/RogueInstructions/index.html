Hi there!


Putting this rack together I learned some stuff that might you when you build yours.

The first section is the building approach I found to be both safest and easiest.

The second section is a step by step guide with some tips.

Let’s go through the approach of building the RML-390F FLAT FOOT MONSTER LITE RACK!

To safely build the rack ideally you need two people but it can be done with one.

To minimize risk of injury, build the rack horizontally.

This will allow the furthest a part can fall to be 4 ft instead of 7.5 ft.

Building horizontally will allow most parts to be attached flat on the ground.

Occasionally you’ll need to lift the rack slightly to put in a bolt.

You’ll need at least 2* people to safely lift the rack.


*One person can do it, but they need to be comfortable tilting the rack upright–the rack weighs ~300lbs at 7.5 foot tall.

What Tools do You Need?

Pliers


Spanner to fit a 1 inch bolt


Stepping stool if you can't reach 7.5 ft from standing
